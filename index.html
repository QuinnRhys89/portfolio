Quinn Anderson

Web Developer + Creative

Hi, I'm Quinn!

I'm a front-end web developer who is passionate about the intersection of technology, art and equity. With a background in Radio and Television Arts, I’m interested in all of the ways that web development can be harnessed as a medium for social innovation and digital storytelling.

I love solving programmatic tasks and have keen eye for design. I strive to create clean, semantic code and am constantly improving my workflow.

When my hands aren’t coding, they can be found strumming an acoustic guitar, balancing a coffee cup on a packed streetcar or coated in chalk before a rock-climbing adventure at Rock Oasis.

I’m excited to connect with other people in tech community whether that be for networking, collaborating or skill-sharing.

Feel free to get in touch!
